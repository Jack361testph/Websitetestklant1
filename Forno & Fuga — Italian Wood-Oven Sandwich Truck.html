Our Story

Born in Tuscany,
driven by passion

Forno & Fuga started as a dream in a small Florentine workshop where we fell in love with the ancient craft of wood-fire baking. We packed that flame into a vintage truck and took it north — bringing the authentic taste of Italian street food to festivals, weddings, and corporate gatherings across the Netherlands.

Every sandwich begins with our signature focaccia, baked live in the wood-fired oven mounted in the truck. We source our meats and cheeses directly from small Italian producers who share our belief that food should be honest, seasonal, and deeply satisfying.

What We Serve

Our Signature Panini

Every sandwich is built on our own wood-oven focaccia and pressed until golden. We keep the menu tight and the quality exceptional.

Our Signature

Il Classico

Prosciutto di Parma, fresh mozzarella di bufala, sun-dried tomatoes, basil pesto, and a drizzle of aged balsamic — pressed golden in our wood oven.

Spicy Pick

La Calabrese

Spicy Calabrian salami, smoked scamorza, roasted red peppers, capers, and nduja butter — bold flavours with a slow fire behind them.

Vegetarian

Il Vegetariano

Grilled aubergine, zucchini, sweet peppers, burrata, walnut tapenade, and herb oil — wood-oven roasted vegetables at their finest.

Fan Favourite

Il Porchetta

Slow-roasted porchetta, herbed ricotta, salsa verde, pickled radicchio, and crispy crackling — the heartbeat of central Italy.

What to Expect

The Forno & Fuga Experience

We don't just cater — we perform. Your guests watch the wood oven glow, smell the bread blister, and receive their sandwich hand-wrapped in wax paper.

🪵

Live Wood-Fire Show

Our oven reaches 400°C. Watching focaccia transform in seconds is half the entertainment — guests gather round before they even order.

🧀

Authentic Italian Ingredients

We import DOP-certified prosciutto, mozzarella di bufala, and nduja directly from partner farms in Emilia-Romagna and Calabria.

🥪

Handmade to Order

No pre-made batches. Each panino is assembled and pressed to order so every customer gets it hot, crispy, and personalised.
